What is laser dentistry?

+

Laser dentistry uses lasers to treat various dental conditions in a more precise manner.

How do dental implants work?

+

Dental implants are surgical components that interface with the bone of the jaw or skull to support a dental prosthesis.

Are clear aligners effective?

+

Clear aligners are effective for mild to moderate teeth misalignment and are a popular alternative to braces.

What is root canal treatment?

+

Root canal treatment is a procedure used to save an infected tooth by removing the damaged pulp and sealing it.
